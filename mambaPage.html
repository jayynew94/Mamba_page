Here's a timeline of Kobe Bryant's basketball career.

  • 1996: Kobe Bryant was drafted by the Charlotte Hornets with the 13th pick. The Hornets traded Kobe to the Los Angeles Lakers.
  • 1997: Kobe Bryant wins the Slam Dunk contest at 18 years old.
  • 2000: Kobe Bryant is joined by coach Phil Jackson and center Shaq wins the NBA championship against the Indiana Pacers.
  • 2001: Kobe Bryant wins championship against the Philadelphia 76ers.
  • 2002: Kobe Bryant wins championship against New Jersey Nets.
  • 2004: Kobe Bryant and Shaq part ways after losing the Finals.
  • 2005: Kobe Bryant is joined by Phil Jackson Again
  • 2006: Kobe Bryant scores career high 81 points against the Toronto Raptors.
  • 2008: Kobe Bryant becomes an Olympian winning the gold medal for USA.
  • 2009: Kobe Bryant wins the NBA Championship against Orlando Magic.
  • 2010: Kobe Bryant wins the NBA championship against the Boston Celtics.
  • 2012: Kobe Bryant wins his final olympic medal.
  • 2016: Kobe Bryant retires from basketball.
